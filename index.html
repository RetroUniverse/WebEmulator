Why not RetroArch?

Fair question. RetroArch is brilliant software — it plays more systems than anything else and its power-user features are legendary. But it's built for one kind of player. RetroUniverse is built for everyone else.

// CREDIT WHERE IT'S DUE
We're not saying RetroArch is bad. It's genuinely brilliant software. If you're on a beefy desktop PC, want 240Hz CRT shader chains, and you enjoy tuning frame delay — stay with RetroArch. That's its home turf and it wins there. RetroUniverse is for the other 95% of retro gaming: a Chromebook at lunch, an iPad on the couch, a school laptop, a friend's PC. The best emulator is the one you can actually open.
