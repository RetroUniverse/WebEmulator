🕹️ Homebrew Games

Curated, 100% legal homebrew games made by indie developers who released them for free. One click to play — no ROM upload needed.

About these games: Every title below was released by its developer as free/freeware. ROMs are loaded directly from the official retrobrews homebrew collection on GitHub. If you're one of the developers and want your game removed, contact us and we'll take it down immediately.

We're not saying Web RetroArch is bad. It's the official browser build from the team that made RetroArch. If you already know RetroArch, want the classic menu, and need access to every libretro core — go use it, it's genuinely good. RetroUniverse is for everyone else: the person who just wants to play a game without learning a config system. No cores to pick, no menus to navigate, no cache to clear when things break. 🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

RetroUniverse respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What we collect

Account data (if you sign up): email address, display name, and an encrypted password. We use Firebase Authentication, operated by Google, to handle this.

Game data: your play history, favorites, save states, screenshots, and statistics. These are stored in Firebase Firestore + Firebase Storage and tied to your account.

Technical data: your browser stores game ROMs you upload, save files, and your control mappings locally. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly sync it.

Chat messages: if you use Community Chat, your messages and display name are visible to other users and stored on Firebase.

What we don't collect

We don't run third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or fingerprinting scripts. We don't sell your data. We don't have access to the ROMs you play — they stay in your browser.

Third-party services we rely on

Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, file storage. Google's privacy policy applies to data they handle on our behalf.
GitHub (raw.githubusercontent.com) — we fetch free homebrew game ROMs from the retrobrews collection when you click Play Now.
EmulatorJS CDN — we load the emulator engine from cdn.emulatorjs.org.

Cookies & local storage

We use browser localStorage and IndexedDB to remember your login, save your games, and store your preferences. These are stored on your device, not sent to us. Firebase Auth may set cookies to keep you logged in.

Your rights

You can delete your account at any time by contacting us (link in footer). On request, we'll delete your account data, chat messages, and any content tied to your profile. You can also clear your local browser data via your browser's settings.

Children

RetroUniverse is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have, contact us and we'll delete it.

Changes

We may update this policy. When we do, we'll change the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes will be announced on the homepage.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Use the Contact Us link in the footer.
